Rubik's Cube Class For Beginners

This class focuses on learning how to solve the Rubik’s cube using the CFOP method. No previous experience is required. The course is around 12 sessions. This is an excellent session for beginners (3rd grade and up).


To go alongside the lessons, I have created the videos below. These videos are in depth tutorials on all of the different steps in order to solve the Rubik’s Cube! My students can use these videos to practice and review the content of previous lessons. Feel free to share these videos with your friends and family!

Getting Started

Learning to solve the Rubik's Cube starts with Cube Theory Basics!

Cubing Notation is very important to know in order to learn the rest of the steps quickly!

Cross

Building the Daisy formation is an easy and fun strategy to set you up for making the Cross!

Building the Cross is the first step out of CFOP.

First Two Layers

After inserting the corners, you will have solved the white side!

Once you have solved the edges, you will have completed the First Two Layers of the Rubik's Cube!

Orienting Last Layer

The Yellow Cross is very different from the White Cross since we have to make sure not to mess anything up!

You can orient the corners of any possibility using just the Sune algorithm!

Permuting Last Layer

Although you only need to learn one algorithm for this step, it is the longest one of all!

Once you have solved the yellow edge pieces, you will have solved the cube!
